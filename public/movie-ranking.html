The Best Movies According to Alicenk

Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Bourne Identity

This is a great movie as it involves the CIA trying to kill their own agent, but Jason Bourne was an outstanding recruit where he was able to fight back

Naruto

This is my favourite anime as it involves powers that are unimaginable by your average human and really gets you thinking and emotionally involved!!

Prison Break

A great television series involving Michael Scofield who tattoos his whole body with a prison blueprint to use it in order to save and escape the prison with his brother.
